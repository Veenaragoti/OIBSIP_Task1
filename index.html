About NS
spa and theraphy center about us

And…relax. As you enter Natural Spa, a sense of calm transports you.

Rooted in a holistic approach to wellbeing, our luxury spa treatments are more than skin deep. Your body is revitalised, mind soothed and spirit lifted – leaving you feeling bright and beautiful long after your spa day has come to an end.

Every formula we use is British-made by the skincare experts at Bamford and Oskia London for our body treatments and luxury facials. Infused with pure, natural and – where possible – organic extracts, our treatments harness the healing power of nature. And our highly trained therapists will make sure every moment is tailored to your needs.
